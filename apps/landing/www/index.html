About HTTPS warnings

When you open Kolibri or NextCloud your browser may show a security warning because this portal uses a self-signed certificate.

  1. On the warning page, tap or click Advanced or Details.
  2. Choose Proceed to kolibri.him-edu.local (unsafe) or Proceed to nextcloud.him-edu.local (unsafe) to continue.

This is expected on this offline, local network. Do not ignore certificate warnings on public or unknown networks.
